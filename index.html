WHAT SHOULD I DO IF I GET A NOTICE OF VIOLATION?

1. SEARCH

Enter your notice of violation in our search below

2. VERIFY

The first thing you should do is to look at the video of the violation to determine Whether it is your vehicle.

3. VISIT

Once you have determined that it is indeed your vehicle, you may go to the Traggic and Parking Management Office

4. EXEMPTION

If you were giving way to emergency vehicules, an exemption would apply. Refer to FAQs or the help center for further questions
