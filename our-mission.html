OUR MISSION

Based on the 2015 census conducted by the Philippines Statistics Office, Paranaque City had a population of 665,500 with a projected growth of 6% by 2019. Unfortunately, Paranaque City's rooads have not matched this exponential growth with Doctor A. Santos Avenue being the only major road which bisects the city. Due to the major traffic congestion and safety risks this has created - discipline is therefore the only answer for a safer Paranaque!

SAFETY

Unkown to many of the city's residents, Paranaque's main thoroughfare, Dr. A. Santos Avenue is considered to be the MOST dangerous road in the Philippines, yes more than EDSA or Commonwealth Avenue aka the Killer Highway.

According to Thinking Machines, a large data company that studied traffic data derived from MMDA, Dr. A. Santos was the most dangerous road for Drivers and the fifth most dangerous road for pedestrians if you consider the number of accidents per 10,000 residents.

Traffic Congestion

Since congestion is apparent in our beloved city and no new roads are being built, vehicular traffic within Paranaque City especially Dr. A. Santos Avenue, must be free from obstruction and should move orderly to ensure the smooth flow of traffic.

Enforcement of traffic laws is very vital in this important road. Unfortunately, manually apprehending motorists will necissitate the stopping of the apprehended vehicle, thus chocking the alredy congested Dr. A. Santos Avenue. That is why the No Contact Apprehension Program is implementaed in this area so that discipline is implemented without chocking the streets.
