OUR MISSION

Based on the 2015 census conducted by the Philippines Statistics Office, Paranaque City had a population of 665,500 with a projected growth of 6% by 2019. Unfortunately, Paranaque City's rooads have not matched this exponential growth with Doctor A. Santos Avenue being the only major road which bisects the city. Due to the major traffic congestion and safety risks this has created - discipline is therefore the only answer for a safer Paranaque!

TRAFFIC CONGESTION

Since congestion is apparent in our beloved city and no new roads are being built, vehicular traffic within Paranaque City especially Dr. A. Santos Avenue, must be free from obstruction and should move orderly to ensure the smooth flow of traffic.

Enforcement of traffic laws is very vital in this impirtant road. Unfortunately, manually apprehending motorists will necessitate the stopping of the apprehended behicule, this choking the already congested Dr. A. Santos Avenue. This is why the No Contact Apprehension Program is implemented in this area so that discipline is implemented without chocking the streets.

FUNDING & EXPENSES

The city has not spent a single centavo for the implemetnation of this cost effective project. The whole programme is funded through the proceeds that are collected. As motorists become more careful, the city and residents could see a reduction in emergency cases and other expenses related to vehicular accidents.
